Our Mission

We hope to provide you with the world's best mercenaries and weapons ranging from armored tanks to assualt rifles, grenades and surface to air missiles

Tanks

Our tanks consist of:

    • Light tanks:
      Maneuverable vehicles with compact dimensions, good concealment, and view range, but with poor armoring and firepower.
      Medium tanks:
      Versatile units, quite maneuverable, with considerable firepower and armoring, and often with a good view range.
      Heavy tanks.
      Vehicles with low maneuverability, but decent armor and powerful gun that can endure and cause considerable damage.
